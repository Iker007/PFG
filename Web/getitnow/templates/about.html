About us

Connecting consumers to local restaurants


Get It Now.com is a leading global online food delivery marketplace, connecting consumers and restaurants through its platforms. With over 10,000 connected restaurants offering consumers a wide variety of food choice.

Located in Bilbao, the company was created in December 2021, by Iker Silva, student at the University of Deusto.


Our marketplace benefits


As an online food delivery marketplace, Get It Now.com facilitates the online ordering, payment and occasionally, fulfilment of orders. We utilise a hybrid model which builds on our marketplace heritage (where restaurants do their own delivery) with logistics capability (for select restaurants without their own delivery capabilities). This model ensures consumers have the widest possible variety of restaurants to order from.

Consumers can order via our website. We derive our revenue principally from the commissions we charge restaurants based on the Gross Transaction Value (GTV) of the food ordered through our marketplace and, to a lesser extent, from other services such as online payment services, sales of merchandise and packaging, and promoted placement. In addition, we also derive revenue from delivery fees charged to consumers on orders for which Get It Now.com is responsible for the Delivery.
